01 — The Problem

Enterprises are shipping AI agents they can’t vouch for.

A polished demo says nothing about how an agent behaves on its worst day — with an adversarial user, an edge-case question, or a compliance trap. Today that gap is invisible until a customer finds it.

01Failures are invisible until they’re public.Bad advice, hallucinated facts, leaked data, and off-policy answers surface in production, not in review.
02Manual QA doesn’t scale.Humans can’t re-test every agent, every version, against every risk — cost and coverage collapse.
03No defensible “go / no-go.”There is no single, trusted answer to “is this agent safe enough to deploy?”
04Regulatory exposure is rising.When something goes wrong, there’s no audit trail to show what was tested and why it shipped.
02 — The Solution

Verity turns opinion into evidence.

It automatically stress-tests every agent, scores it across six trust pillars, issues a clear deploy verdict, watches it in production, and closes the loop by proposing fixes when it drifts — all in one control center.

STEP 01

Stress-test

Realistic synthetic users probe the agent with hard, adversarial, and edge-case conversations — at machine scale.

STEP 02

Score & gate

Expert “judges” grade every answer and produce a single go / no-go deploy gate.

STEP 03

Monitor

Live observability tracks quality, spend, and drift the moment the agent is in front of real traffic.

STEP 04

Self-heal

When quality breaches, Verity finds the root cause and proposes a fix for a human to approve.

03 — How It Works

One pipeline, from question to evidence.

Every evaluation follows the same five stages — so results are repeatable, comparable, and auditable across agents and versions.

01

Persona

Define who tests the agent — novice, adversarial, frustrated, compliance-bait.

02

Question

Generate hard, on-topic scenarios that push the agent’s limits.

03

Run

Drive the real agent through full multi-turn conversations.

04

Verdict

A jury of judges scores each answer across the six trust pillars.

05

Evidence

Package the proof — scores, transcripts, and the deploy gate.
