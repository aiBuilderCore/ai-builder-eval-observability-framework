Typography.

02 · display · body · mono

Three families. Space Grotesk carries headings and labels (its tighter geometry reads as confident at large sizes). Geist carries body copy and forms (modern, neutral, comfortable at 16px / 1.7). JetBrains Mono carries data, IDs, version stamps, and tokens.

Aa — Space Grotesk 500 / 600 / 700 display · headings · labels
Persona Lab

A small, deliberate fiction — cast a character, give it goals, give it edge-cases, then keep grading the agent against it until the agent holds up.

Aa — Geist 400 / 500 / 600 / 700 body · forms · long copy
The roster reads.

Body type carries forms, tables, hints, and the long edge-case lists. It needs to stay quiet under 500 words of dense prose without becoming generic — Geist's wider apertures keep it readable at 14px while staying neutral enough to host the violet accent without clashing.
