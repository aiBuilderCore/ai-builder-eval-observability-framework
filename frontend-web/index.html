Verity

Verity checks that your AI agents are safe and reliable before they go live. It creates realistic test users, asks them tricky questions, sends those questions to your agent, and lets independent judges score every answer. It keeps watching quality after launch, stores a complete audit ready record for reviewers, and automatically turns any flagged problem into a fix before it reaches your customers. One connected trail, from the first test all the way to go live.
