The closed feedback loop

spec →

Five stages run in sequence — Observe, Evaluate, Curate, Deploy, Attest — with two cross-cutting quality checks (Calibrate, Identify) that can fire from any stage. The tab strip at the top follows this order.

Sequential stages

1 Observe Live ingest Prod traces arrive over OTLP under the same span contract batches use. 2 Evaluate Batches Inline judges score every run. Four-tuple lineage, pass-rate vs prev. 5 Curate Datasets Promote failing runs into versioned, health-tracked training / eval sets. 6 Deploy Gate Baseline vs candidate. All configured signals must clear or BLOCK. 7 Attest Evidence EU AI Act · SR 11-7 · FINRA. Signed, config_hash-pinned packs.

Cross-cutting checks — fire from any stage

3 Calibrate Judge κ vs human gold Do your judges still agree with human raters? Feeds the gate's trust check. 4 Identify Trajectory drift Failure modes on the shape of the trace, not scalars.
